About Me

Hey there,

My name is Dikson Manganye. I am Web Developer with experience in wireframing and coding interactive websites and applications. I enjoy the fast-paced environment of the tech industry.


I am a detail-oriented and committed developer, with a get-it-done, on-time and high-quality product spirit. I have experience defining requirements, designing, implementing, testing and delivering front-end web applications using a variety of programming languages and technologies.


Out side of being a developer, I enjoy drawing, gym and hiking.
